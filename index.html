Our Story

Crafting Beauty Since 1985

Founded in 1985, Chhaparwal Bandhu has been a trusted name in the world of artificial jewellery. What started as a small family shop has grown into a beloved jewellery destination, serving thousands of happy customers across India.

We believe that every woman deserves to sparkle, regardless of budget. Our collection blends traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary designs, offering something beautiful for every occasion — from daily wear to grand bridal ceremonies.

✓ Skin-safe, hypoallergenic materials
✓ Traditional craftsmanship & modern designs
✓ Custom bridal & festive orders
